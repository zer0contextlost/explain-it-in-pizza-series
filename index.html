About the Series

Why Pizza?

Because AI is complicated, and pizza is universally understood. Tokens are toppings. Attention is a chef deciding what matters. Fine-tuning is teaching Gordon Ramsay to make your grandma's sauce.

Each episode is a fully interactive experience — not slides, not videos, not walls of text. You click things. Things animate. Concepts click. 🍕

No math degree required. No prior ML knowledge needed. Just an appetite for learning.
